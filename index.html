Our Story

Built Where
Blues Was Born

Acadian Red was born out of frustration. After years of watching great amps get dinged, dusty, and damaged by covers that were never really designed — just manufactured — we decided to do it right.

Rooted in Louisiana's tradition of building things that last, every Acadian Red cover is a deliberate product — not an afterthought. We obsess over fit, material quality, and construction so you don't have to think about it.

Our launch series targets the five most common amp sizes in the world. More sizes and limited custom runs are already in development.
