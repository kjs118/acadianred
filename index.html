Our Story

Built With
Purpose

Acadian Red was born out of frustration. After years of watching great amps get dinged, dusty, and soaked at gigs, it became clear most covers were never really designed for how musicians actually use their gear.

This started in Louisiana's live music scene, where tight stages, late nights, and the occasional spilled drink are part of the job. We needed something reliable. Something that could take a hit, clean up easy, and still look right on stage.

Every Acadian Red cover is built with that in mind. We focus on fit, material quality, and construction so your gear stays protected without getting in the way.

Our launch series covers the most common amp sizes used by working musicians, with additional sizes in development.
